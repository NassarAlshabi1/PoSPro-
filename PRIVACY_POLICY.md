# Privacy Policy for PoSPro

*Last updated: January 24, 2026*

## 1. Introduction

PoSPro is a decentralized, secure Point of Sale (PoS) solution developed by **REChain Network Solutions** using Flutter, Dart, and related open-source technologies.

This Privacy Policy explains how data is processed, stored, and protected when using PoSPro software, including client applications, self-hosted backends, and optional integrations.

PoSPro follows the principles of **privacy by design**, **data minimization**, and **operator control**.

## 2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to:

* PoSPro client applications (mobile, desktop, terminal)
* Self-hosted PoSPro servers or nodes
* Open-source repositories under REChain Network Solutions related to PoSPro

This policy does **not** apply to third-party services, payment providers, banking systems, or fiscal operators that may be integrated by merchants or operators.

## 3. Key Privacy Principles

* **No mandatory central server**: PoSPro can be deployed in fully self-hosted or decentralized environments.
* **Merchant-controlled data**: Transaction and customer data remain under the control of the merchant or system operator.
* **Minimal data collection**: Only data strictly necessary for transaction processing is handled.
* **Transparency**: PoSPro source code is publicly available for audit.

## 4. Information We Collect

### 4.1 Information You Provide

When you use PoSPro, we may collect the following information:

* **Account Information**: Email address, business name, phone number, and address when you create an account
* **Business Information**: Business category, company details, and subscription information
* **User Information**: User roles, permissions, and profile information for team members
* **Product Information**: Product names, descriptions, prices, and inventory details
* **Transaction Data**: Sales, purchases, and payment information
* **Financial Data**: Bank account information, payment method details (processed by third-party payment processors)

### 4.2 Information Collected Automatically

* **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
* **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the app, including features used and actions taken
* **Log Data**: Diagnostic, technical, and error information related to app performance
* **Location Data**: Approximate location information inferred from your IP address (for analytics purposes)

### 4.3 Information We Do Not Collect

By default, REChain Network Solutions does **not** collect:

* Customer personal identification data
* Payment card numbers or CVV codes
* Biometric data
* Advertising or tracking identifiers
* Behavioral analytics for marketing purposes

## 5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

* Provide, maintain, and improve the PoSPro services
* Process transactions and manage your business operations
* Communicate with you about your account and services
* Send you technical notices, updates, and security alerts
* Respond to your requests and provide customer support
* Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 6.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

* **Payment Processors**: For processing payments (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)
* **Cloud Storage Providers**: For data backup and storage
* **Analytics Services**: For understanding app usage and improving our services
* **Email Service Providers**: For sending you communications

### 6.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

### 6.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

* Encryption of data in transit and at rest
* Regular security assessments and penetration testing
* Access controls and authentication mechanisms
* Secure coding practices and regular code reviews

Despite these measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When data is no longer needed, we securely delete it in accordance with our retention policies.

## 9. Your Rights and Choices

### 9.1 Account Information

You can review and update your account information through the app settings.

### 9.2 Data Export

You can export your business data at any time through the Data Export feature in the app settings.

### 9.3 Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data through the Delete Account option in the app settings. See our Account Deletion Instructions for more information.

## 10. Children's Privacy

PoSPro is not intended for use by children under the age of 16 and does not knowingly collect information from children under this age.

## 11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

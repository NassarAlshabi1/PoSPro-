# Google Play Account Deletion Instructions for PoSPro

*Last updated: January 24, 2026*

## Account Deletion Process for Google Play Users

In compliance with Google Play Developer Policy Requirements, this document provides clear instructions on how to delete your PoSPro account.

### How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your PoSPro account at any time by following these steps:

1. Open the PoSPro app on your Android device
2. Navigate to **Settings** from the main menu
3. Select **Advanced Settings**
4. Scroll down to the **Data & Storage** section
5. Tap on **Delete Account**
6. Read the warning message carefully
7. Confirm your decision by tapping **Delete**

### What Happens When You Delete Your Account

* All your business data will be permanently deleted
* All user accounts associated with your business will be removed
* All product, inventory, and transaction data will be erased
* Your subscription will be cancelled
* You will be logged out of the app

### Data Retention

We may retain some information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

* Financial records for tax and accounting purposes (as required by law)
* Transaction logs for security and fraud prevention
* Information needed to comply with legal obligations

### Alternative Method: Contact Support

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, you can contact our support team directly:

* Email: support@pospro.com
* Subject: Account Deletion Request

Please include the following information in your request:
* Your registered email address
* Your business name
* Reason for deletion (optional)

Our support team will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving it.

### Data Portability

Before deleting your account, you can export your data using the Data Export feature in the app settings.

### Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions about this account deletion process, please contact us at:

* Email: support@pospro.com
